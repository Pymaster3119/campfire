# Campfire Game — Instruction Manual

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **A / D** | Move left / right |
| **W / S** | Climb up / down ladders |
| **Space** | Jump |
| **Left Click (hold)** | Mine / break tiles |
| **B** | Toggle build mode |
| **Left Click (build mode)** | Place stone tile |
| **T** | Place turret at mouse cursor |
| **U** | Upgrade nearby turret (costs wood) |
| **R** | Restart / reset the game |

## Stats

Hank has four stats that decay over time:

- **Health** — Drops faster when hungry or dirty. Reach 0 and you die.
- **Hunger** — Decays the fastest. Mining costs 5 hunger per block.
- **Hygiene** — Low hygiene accelerates health decay.
- **Education** — Passive stat.

## Resources

Mined from underground tiles:

| Resource | Use |
|----------|-----|
| Stone | General resource |
| Bronze | Crafting material |
| Iron | Crafting material |
| Gunpowder | Crafting material |
| Diamond | Rare crafting material |
| Medkit | Heals health (dropped by turret kills too) |
| Tile | Used to place stone blocks (start with 50) |
| Wood | Used to upgrade turrets |

## Building

1. Press **B** to enter build mode
2. Left-click to place a stone tile (costs 1 tile)
3. Placed tiles are solid — you can stand on them and they block movement
4. Press **B** again to exit build mode

## Turrets

- Press **T** to place a turret at your mouse cursor
- Turrets auto-target and kill the nearest zombie in range
- Press **U** near a turret to upgrade it (max level 3)

| Level | Range | Fire Rate | Upgrade Cost |
|-------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | 60px | 1.0s | — |
| 2 | 80px | 0.8s | 2 wood |
| 3 | 100px | 0.6s | 4 wood |

- Turret kills have a 10% chance to drop a medkit

## Zombies

- Zombies spawn in waves (hordes) and chase Hank
- They deal damage on contact
- Use turrets and terrain to defend yourself

## Tips

- Mine underground for resources but watch your hunger
- Place stone tiles to build walls and platforms
- Set up turrets near your base to defend against zombie hordes
- Keep an eye on your stats — if health hits 0, you die (press R to restart)
